About Us

Welcome to UNN MALL



This is an online product delivery information management system tailored for university environments, with a focus on efficient book delivery. Traditional manual methods of record-keeping are prone to errors and time-consuming, highlighting the need for a streamlined and transparent system.

The web application aims to address challenges such as lack of visibility into delivery status, order mix-ups, communication gaps, and delays. Users will be able to easily place orders, track delivery status, and receive updates, thereby reducing waiting times and minimizing disruptions. The significance of this project lies in its potential to enhance the overall campus experience by providing a user-friendly platform that integrates seamlessly into existing university infrastructure, contributing to improved efficiency.
